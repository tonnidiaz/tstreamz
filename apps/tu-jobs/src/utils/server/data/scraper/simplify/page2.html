Job Description

ROLE PURPOSE

Implement and maintain site specific safety, health and environmental requirements in line with Company Policy and Procedure, client site SHE requirements and legal requirements for designated contracts.

MAIN OUTPUTS
  • Create and maintain health and safety files for designated contracts
  • Generate site SHE related reports and communicate to Ops Manager and client
  • Create and maintain site risk assessments, Safe Work Procedures, Planned Task Observations
  • Monitor PPE, legal appointments, competencies, fire equipment, medicals
  • Conduct Planned Task Observations
  • Report, investigate incidents, generate report, communicate, follow up
  • Conduct SHE site inspections and monitor and update registers, checklists, medicals, safety related training matrix
  • Attend client site safety meetings
  • Establish Prestige site safety committee, chair quarterly meetings, minutes, follow up on actions
  • SHE Communication - SHE risks with site employees, SHE awareness, conduct safety related informal training, SHE toolbox talks, incident lessons learnt

QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS
The Applicant must meet the following requirements:
  • Matric (Senior Certificate)
  • Valid SA Drivers’ License
  • Risk assessments, incident investigation
  • Samtrac, Incident Investigation, Legal Liability, Fall Protection Plan Developer, HIRA, Root Cause Analysis, Bidvest Prestige activities in all industries
  • MS Word, MS Excel, MS Powerpoint, MS Outlook (Intermediate skill level)
  • Knowledge of OHS and COID Acts, Industry SANS standards related to Bidvest Prestige service offerings
