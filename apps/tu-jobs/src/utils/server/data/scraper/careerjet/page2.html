Office Admin / Receptionist - Brackenfell 

 

Our client is a dynamic, service- and solution-driven company that supplies concrete-related consumables and PPE to the Building and Civils Industry. They are looking for an enthusiastic and confident Administrator / Receptionist to join their team. 

Key Responsibilities: 

  • Greet and assist clients, suppliers, and visitors in a friendly and professional manner. 
  • Manage incoming calls and emails, directing inquiries appropriately. 
  • Handle general administrative tasks, including filing, data entry, and document management. 
  • Process and capture orders accurately and efficiently. 
  • Match purchase orders with invoices and assist with basic financial administration. 
  • Assist with invoicing, stock-related admin, and order processing. 
  • Coordinate with suppliers and associates to ensure smooth daily operations. 
  • Maintain a tidy and welcoming reception area. 
  • Provide administrative support to the sales and operations teams as needed. 

Requirements: 

  • Personality: A go-getter with a friendly, bubbly personality who can interact well with a  variety of personalities. 
  • Communication: Confident communicator with the ability to engage effectively with clients, suppliers, and associates. 
  • Languages: Must be fluent in English and have a good understanding of Afrikaans. Additional languages will be beneficial. 
  • Computer Literacy: Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook) and general computer skills. 
  • Attention to Detail: Ability to accurately match purchase orders and invoices. 
  • Organized &amp; Proactive: Ability to multitask, prioritize tasks, and work independently in a fast-paced environment. 

To apply, please send your application to goldenthreadrec@outlook.com and quote the position you are applying for in the subject line. If you do not hear from us within two weeks, please accept that your application has unfortunately been unsuccessful.
